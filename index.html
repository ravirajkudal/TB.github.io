How to Download iOS 15 and iPadOS 15 Beta 7

If you are an iPhone or iPad user and you want to try the new iOS 15 on your devices, then you are in the right place. Apple has released the iOS 15 and iPadOS 15 Beta 7 recently. In this article, we are going to tell you how to download iOS 15 and iPadOS 15 beta 7. You just have to follow some steps and you will be able to download iOS 15 and iPadOS beta 7. The latest Beta 7 brings more bug fixes and brings stability to more devices testing out the software. As the beta releases are getting pushed, we are really close to the final release of the iOS 15 Production Release. Soon Apple might speed up the development process of the software itself.


How to Enroll in iOS Beta Program?

If you don’t know how to enroll in the iOS beta program then we have got you covered just follow some easy steps to enroll. Here’s how to enroll in the iOS beta program: Step 1. Visit www.developer.apple.com and log in using your Apple ID. Step 2. You will find all the programs in which you can enroll. Step 3. After you have logged in with your Apple ID go to the Downloads section, then scroll down to iOS 15 beta and tap Install Profile. Step 4. Now go to Settings then tap on General> Profile and tap on the iOS 15 beta profile there. Step 5. Tap on the install on the top right and the installation will start. Note: Before downloading and installing the iOS 15 beta profile do read the developer consent form. Make sure you are okay with all the information Apple will be taking from you. Step 6. After you are done with all the steps restart your iPhone. Step 7. To get the update, just follow the aarbovementioned how to download iOS 15 beta 7 steps and you will be able to enjoy the iOS 15 Beta without any issues.
